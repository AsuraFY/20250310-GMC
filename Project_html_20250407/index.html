Who are the BTS?

BTS is a seven-member group formed by Big Hit Entertainment (HYBE) in 2013. They are known for blending K-pop with hip-hop, R&B, EDM, and pop, along with deep, personal lyrics.

The members are:

  1. RM (Kim Namjoon)
  2. Jin (Kim Seokjin)
  3. Suga (Min Yoongi)
  4. J-Hope (Jung Hoseok)
  5. Jimin (Park Jimin)
  6. V (Kim Taehyung)
  7. Jungkook (Jeon Jungkook)
